MShare Support

What is MShare?

MShare is a handy tool that helps you share music between Apple Music and Spotify. Imagine you have a song you love on Apple Music, but your friend only uses Spotify. MShare takes the link from Apple Music and finds the equivalent song on Spotify, generating a shareable link for them to enjoy!

How to Use MShare

  1. Open the MShare app.
  2. While MShare doesn't directly appear in the iOS Share Sheet, you can still easily share music links.
  3. Steps:
    1. Find the music link you want to share (e.g., in Apple Music, Spotify, or a website).
    2. Look for the "Share" button or menu option within that app or website.
    3. Open the MShare app. (You can use the MShare app directly if you add it to the share dialog)
    4. Paste the copied link into the MShare app.
    5. Select your friend's preferred music platform (e.g., Spotify).
    6. MShare will search for the matching song and generate a shareable link.
  4. Benefits of MShare: Having MShare installed offers a streamlined approach to sharing music links, regardless of the source app.

Supported Platforms (Currently)

MShare currently supports sharing music between:

  • Apple Music
  • Spotify

We're working on adding support for even more platforms in the future! Stay tuned for updates!
